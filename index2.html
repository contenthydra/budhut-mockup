FAQ

Q. Who is allowed to enter?

A. Adults age 21 and over with a valid ID. See examples of acceptable identification here.

Q. How much can I buy?

A. Once a day, adults age 21 and older may purchase up to one ounce of usable marijuana (flower), 16 ounces of solid edibles, 72 ounces in liquid form, and 7 grams of concentrates.

Q. Where can I consume this?

A. A private home would be the safest location. It is currently illegal to consume cannabis in view of the public.

Q. Can I take this out of state?

A. No. It is illegal to bring cannabis outside of Washington State.

Q. Do you accept debit cards?

A. At this time, we are cash-only. We do, however, provide an ATM inside of each of our store.

Q. What is the return policy?

A. We currently do not accept returns of any kind.

Q. What is the return policy?

A. We currently do not accept returns of any kind.

Q. Can photos be taken inside?

A. Absolutely! We’d love for you to take photos of our shop, and please don’t forget to tag us on social media, so we can see the images later on!

Q. Veteran discount?

A. Thank you for your service! We provide a 10% Veteran discount with Military ID. Some exclusions may apply, product or brand will vary by location.

Q. Senior Discount?

A. Senior citizens may enjoy 10% off their purchase every Sunday! Please check our location-specific menus for availability, as product and brands may vary by location. Offer applies to select items only.

Q. Pets Allowed Inside?

A. Well-behaved service animals are permitted inside.
